# Product

## Register

product

## Users
An active developer and learner who uses spaced repetition to acquire English vocabulary and technical concepts (AI, Docker, programming). They need a highly aesthetic, creative, and motivating interface to inspire daily study and visual retention.

## Product Purpose
A highly visual Spaced Repetition PKM combining a main-screen canvas timeline, knowledge graph, and flashcard review system. Success looks like frictionless learning, clear tracking of memory health, and a beautiful timeline interface that prompts immediate, focused engagement.

## Brand Personality
Tối giản - sáng tạo - truyền cảm hứng (Minimalist - Creative - Inspiring)

## Anti-references
- Cluttered SaaS dashboards with heavy shadow cards and unnecessary metrics.
- Low-contrast design elements (such as light gray text on light background) that make reading hard.
- Distracting decorative animations or "AI-slop" features that serve no functional purpose.

## Design Principles
- **Focus on the Core (Tập trung vào trọng tâm)**: Make critical information large and clear. Every UI element must serve the learning workflow; avoid feature creep and visual clutter.
- **Notion-like layouts & Quizlet-like creation**: Keep layout structure extremely clean, aligned, and modular, while making flashcard creation and review feel tactile, straightforward, and engaging.
- **Inspired Motion (Chuyển động truyền cảm hứng)**: Add creative, fluid micro-interactions and transitions to motivate exploration, while keeping the background stable to support deep concentration.
- **High-contrast Visual Clarity**: Rely on strong typographic hierarchy and clean color blocking rather than decorative containers. Use bold contrast for primary texts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High color contrast (minimum WCAG AA 4.5:1 ratio) to ensure effortless readability.
- Support smooth canvas zooming and panning without motion sickness, respecting `prefers-reduced-motion` settings.
